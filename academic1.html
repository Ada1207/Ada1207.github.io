• Extended Project Qualification Project

Standing out in the highly competitive toy market, Pop Mart has achieved remarkable success through its unique marketing strategy. Since its inception in 2010, Pop Mart has attracted a large number of young consumers, especially millennials, with its cute design and affordable prices. Through continuous innovation and collaboration with renowned designers, Pop Mart not only meets the entertainment needs of consumers, but also builds a strong brand presence on a global scale.

Introduction

Pop Mart stands as a global frontier and trailblazer in the toy industry, serving as a brand of entertainment with billions of followers, especially among teenagers. The driving force behind the explosive growth of Pop Mart includes economic factors like long work hours, low pay, and high housing prices, making affordable entertainment options attractive.

Marketing Strategies

Pop Mart’s key strategy involves selling blind boxes with identical exteriors but different contents inside, offering affordable and appealing toy figures. The brand experienced a significant surge in sales, with revenue increasing over 230% from 2017 to 2018.

Financial Analysis

This section analyzes Pop Mart's profitability using annual reports. Key indicators such as gross profit margin and net profit margin are discussed, comparing Pop Mart’s financial performance with other toy companies like LEGO and Disney.

Consumer Preferences

Primary research is conducted through questionnaires to investigate consumer preferences. This helps to understand the effectiveness of Pop Mart's marketing strategies and adapt them based on consumer feedback.

Conclusion

The report concludes by summarizing Pop Mart's position in the market, the effectiveness of its marketing strategies, and its financial health, providing insights for future strategic decisions.
